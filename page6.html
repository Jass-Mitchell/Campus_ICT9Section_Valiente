Passenger Tips and Reminders

Our primary concerns are your safety and a better overall experience.Furthermore, our community guidelines have been published in order to make KKK a safe and equitable place for all.

Dos and Dont's when using KKK

✅ Double check booking details. ❌ Placing incorrect information.

Select the appropriate pick-up and drop-off location.

✅ Be their at the pickup time. ❌ Making the driver wait too long.

When you're ready to go, make your reservation. Arrive on time for your ride. Avoid canceling without a valid reason.

✅ Double check your belongings before and after ride. ❌ Immediately leave or enter the vehicle.

Make sure to not forget any needed items or belongings.
