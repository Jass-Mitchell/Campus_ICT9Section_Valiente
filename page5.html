How to book a ride

1.Register

Set up an account using your email address and phone number.

2.Set Route

Type in where you want to be fetched up and where you want to go. Verify the accuracy of the map pins.

3.Select Vehicle

Decide on your favorite mode of transportation (e.g., 4-wheel/car for comfort or 2-wheel/motorcycle for speed).

3.Select Payment

Pick from cash, credit/debit cards, or e-wallets like KKKCash or KKKPay.

4.Apply Promos

To input any applicable discount codes, tap the "Offers" or "Promos" area.

5.Book and Confirm

Press the "Book" or "Confirm" button. The app will look for a driver in the area.
